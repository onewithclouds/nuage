Product Description

Translucent body of a thin bamboo is gracefully standing between Heaven and Earth. A light fragrance is carried with the wind, creating a gentle disturbance…

Sasa is a word of Japanese origin (【笹】, ささ), denoting beautiful ground-cover bamboo that grows to about 1m in height. A distinct feature of Sasa bamboo is the fact that each of its tender branches will develop only one gracious leaf. Sasa leaves are the inspiration behind the design of this beautiful accessory. Also, Sasa will flower only once in 20 years.

Moorigin accessories, through their minimalist and essential nature, lend themselves well to symbolic interpretation. As such, leaves symbolize hope, revival and renewal, representing potency and growth. According to the Chinese tradition, leaves also stand for all of the beings in the universe, hence can be seen as exemplifying people.

Bamboo is a symbol of regeneration, renewal, freedom of spirit and flexibility. It is also the fastest vertically growing plant on the planet with an ability to reach full height in one season. Bamboo’s growth, although remarkable, is always gradual and incremental and is reminiscent of personal continuous improvement and commitment to learning.  Also, bamboo plants might not always look to be as tough as other trees in the forest. Yet, they endure the coldest of winters, hot summers and usually are the only ones standing in the aftermath of a storm. As such, bamboo sends a powerful message that can be summarized in the common wisdom of ‘bend, but do not break’. A message that speaks of a more sophisticated, gentle strength. A strength that does not fight the circumstances, but through flexibility remains standing tall.

Care

Moorigin accessories are quite comfortable and atmospheric, partly because of their low weight. Although, being lightweight these can bend, despite the fact they’re made of steel. Please keep this in mind, to prevent bending your lovely Moorigin accessories. Also, make sure to remove them while bathing, taking shower or swimming, as even stainless steel can be prone to some forms of corrosion when exposed to water or humidity. In the event, your accessories do come in contact with water, simply clean them with a soft cloth.

About Moorigin

The meaningful design concepts behind the visionary contemporary accessories brand Moorigin can be interpreted as inspired by nature, simple and sensual. Moorigin accessories are developed around bare essentials. These essentials include only the material and the design. Inspired by our planet’s living environment, the brand embraces some of nature’s most intricate and enduring forces, such as evolution and structured, orderly chaos. Stemming from a personal vision, unobstructed and uncluttered, Moorigin accessories feel personal, evoking a feeling of atmosphere and sensuality.
