About Francoise Oz

Francoise Oz is an exquisite contemporary wearable sculpture brand with a distinct, unconventional, contemporary aesthetic. The process of creation of jewelry from faience and clay, employed by Francoise Oz is meticulous and labor-intensive. Francoise Oz’s wonderful creations are taking shape through forming raw material, firing, then grinding the fired earthenware pieces by hand and glazing. The resulting pieces are stylish, artistic and absolutely unique. No wonder, Francoise Oz’s faience and ceramic accessories are endorsed by fashion and design connoisseurs worldwide.
