Since the ancient times coral is a traditional gift of protection for the expectant mother or a newborn child. Coral is also associated with emotional healing. Blue coral in particular is believed to have an ability of reconnecting oneself to one’s inner child, providing stability, reducing fear and finding inner peace. Coral is also believed to extend its protection to anyone living, traveling or working by/in the sea. Since coral is of organic origin, it is often thought of as an aid in rekindling one’s relationship with nature and its wonders, stimulating creativity and optimism, as well as enabling it’s bearer to enjoy life to the fullest. It may be the reason why, all around the world, coral has been used as jewelry for at least thousands of years.

Howlite is considered to be a stone of calm and tranquility. A stone that can calm and soothe the emotions, help cope with an overactive mind and achieve a deep and restful sleep. Howlite is believed to reduce conflicts, facilitate communication, promote kindness, compassion and peaceful coexistence.  This truly beautiful and tranquil gem is believed to aid in clarifying one’s mind to develop a more prominent awareness of one’s ambitions and goals. Often referred to as an ‘attunement stone’, Howlite is thought to help expand the horizons and open the mind to wisdom and new knowledge.
