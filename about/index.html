About

Nuage (meaning ‘cloud’ in French) is a showroom taking inspiration in clouds and connecting fashion connoisseurs to designer talent, both clothing and accessories. We put every effort to make your experience of shopping and discovering extraordinary accessories as fluent and comfortable as possible and will gladly take returns. We also take your online privacy seriously. That is why all of your data is transferred through a secure SSL connection during Checkout. If you have any additional questions, feel free to contact us through the chat app on this site, or reach out/send a message on Twitter, Instagram or Facebook.
