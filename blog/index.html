Organic Experience of Jennifer Park’s Wear Ever Jewelry

By onewithclouds | May 16, 2016 | 1 Comment | Jewelry & Accessories

Designer of exclusive contemporary jewelry extraordinaire, Jennifer Jordan Park, is a true talent/mastermind behind the tasteful and exquisite Wear Ever Jewelry brand. The flowing, wavy lines of the brand’s designs add to an organic-like experience of her jewelry pieces. Such is the case with the incredible turquoise and royal blue basse-taille enamel necklace pictured above. The enamel pieces of the necklace are complemented with lapis and emerald gemstones. In line with Wear Ever Jewelry aesthetic, the base of the enamel is carefully prepared, while the silver the pieces are set in is brought to a beautiful contrasting finish, which in this case is matte.  (more…)
